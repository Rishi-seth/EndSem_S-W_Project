Mangalyaan Mission

India's historic Mars Orbiter Mission: a leap forward in space exploration, putting ISRO among the elite space agencies of the world.

About the Mission

Launched on November 5, 2013, by the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO), Mangalyaan was India's first interplanetary mission. It made India the first Asian nation to reach Martian orbit and the first nation globally to do so in its maiden attempt.

Key Highlights

Cost-Effective

At just $74 million, it is one of the most cost-effective Mars missions ever undertaken.

Martian Atmosphere Study

Studied the Martian surface and atmosphere, including methane detection and imaging.

International Milestone

Placed India among the top spacefaring nations, achieving orbit in the first attempt.

Satellite

Legacy of Mangalyaan

Mangalyaan symbolizes India's technological advancement and ingenuity. Its legacy is a source of pride for India and inspiration for future space missions, showcasing ISRO's ability to achieve great milestones with limited resources.
