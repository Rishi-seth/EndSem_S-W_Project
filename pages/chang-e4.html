Chang'e 4 Mission

Exploring the Far Side of the Moon

Mission Overview

Chang'e 4 is China's groundbreaking mission that successfully landed on the far side of the Moon. The mission included a lander and a rover, named *Yutu-2*, designed to study the unique terrain, mineral composition, and the lunar environment.

Mission Highlights

First-Ever Landing

Chang'e 4 made history by becoming the first mission to land on the Moon's far side.

Yutu-2 Rover

The rover explored the Moon's surface, analyzing soil, rocks, and the environment.

Relay Satellite

The Queqiao satellite enabled communication between the far side of the Moon and Earth.

Legacy of Chang'e 4

Chang'e 4 represents a monumental achievement in lunar exploration, pushing the boundaries of space science and inspiring future missions to explore uncharted territories.
